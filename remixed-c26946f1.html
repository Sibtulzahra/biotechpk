Essential Lab Skills for Biotech Students

Master these core techniques to become job-ready and competitive in Pakistan's growing biotech industry.

🧬
Molecular Biology
  • PCR & RT-PCR
  • Gel Electrophoresis
  • DNA Extraction & Purification
  • Southern / Northern Blotting
  • Cloning & Transformation
🔬
Microbiology
  • Aseptic Technique
  • Culture & Staining
  • Antibiotic Sensitivity Testing
  • Fermentation Basics
  • Microscopy (Light/Electron)
🧪
Biochemistry Lab
  • Enzyme Assays
  • Protein Extraction & SDS-PAGE
  • Western Blotting
  • Spectrophotometry
  • Chromatography (HPLC, TLC)
🌱
Plant Biotechnology
  • Tissue Culture & Callus Induction
  • Agrobacterium-Mediated Transfer
  • Transgenic Plant Analysis
  • Phytohormone Application
💻
Bioinformatics
  • NCBI / BLAST Database Use
  • Sequence Alignment (MEGA)
  • Python — Biopython
  • Phylogenetic Tree Building
  • Protein Structure (SWISS-MODEL)
🩺
Medical / Clinical
  • ELISA (Immunoassay)
  • Flow Cytometry Basics
  • Cell Culture & Passaging
  • Biosafety Level Protocols
⚗️
Industrial Biotech
  • Bioreactor Operation
  • Downstream Processing
  • GMP / GLP Compliance
  • Quality Control Testing
📊
Research & Data
  • Statistical Analysis (SPSS, R)
  • Scientific Writing
  • Lab Notebook Documentation
  • Ethics & Biosafety Regulations
